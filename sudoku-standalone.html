How To Play

🎯
The Goal

Fill every cell in the 9×9 grid so that each row, column, and 3×3 box contains the digits 1–9 exactly once.

🔢
Placing Numbers

Tap a cell to select it, then tap a number on the number pad. Blue cells are given clues — you cannot change them.

❌
Mistakes

You can make up to 3 mistakes. Wrong numbers are placed but counted. At 3 mistakes, the game ends!

↩️
Undo

Made a mistake? Hit Undo to revert your last move. Even wrong numbers can be undone!

💡
Hints

You get 3 hints per puzzle. A hint reveals the correct value for the selected cell (or a random empty cell).

📝
Notes Mode

Toggle Notes mode to pencil in candidate numbers. Notes are automatically cleared when you place a number nearby.

⭐
Scoring

Win to earn points! Faster solves and fewer mistakes mean higher scores. Each level raises difficulty by 1%.
